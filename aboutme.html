Wes Bower





Wes Bower is a creator from San Jose, CA, USA who utilizes both tangible and digital media to create physical and cerebral experiences.

His practice is informed by his early roots in digital design and coding, and his development into 3D and 2D animation, sound design, installation art, web design, and photography.

With a rooted interest in art theory, his flexible approach to conceptualization allows for projects to develop their own most effective medium.

Speaking about his process, he says, "To me it is all about the experience, so I am always considering the best way to convey an idea or story. I want the experiences I create to move people and the medium through which that happens can make all the difference".
